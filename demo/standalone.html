📹 TAK Video Viewer — Standalone Test Harness

Exercises the full video engine without WebTAK. Configure your Restreamer, then open feeds.

1 · Point at your TAK Restreamer

Set this once (persisted to localStorage). Or click Add Stream → Settings.

2 · Open streams

Notes

• Raw rtsp://, rtsps://, srt://, rtmp:// feeds require the Restreamer (MediaMTX) to repackage them — the plugin plays :8888/<path>/index.m3u8 (HLS) or :8889/<path>/whep (WebRTC).
• A direct .m3u8 URL plays with no Restreamer.
• Drag a window by its title bar; resize from the bottom-right corner.
